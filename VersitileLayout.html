Washington, DC Activities

Washington in the District of Columbia is a vibrant city with so much to offer.

As our national capital, history buffs will of course enjoy visiting the White House, Capitol building, and the National Mall.

However, Washington has fun for everyone! Here, you can take a bike ride along the Potomac, listen to a Lil Wayne concert, and see a Broadway show! (you don't have to be in New York!)
